About Us

Who Are We?

The Pitt Ballet Club is a student run organization at the University of Pittsburgh which offers opportunities for everyone to dance, ranging in skill level from beginner to advanced.

Who Can Join?

All students at Pitt are welcome to join the club. No prior dance experience is necessary. If you are interested, please feel free to fill out this interest form to be added to our email list.

Our Classes

We offer a masterclass once a week which varies in style and level. Everyone is welcome to come to this class regardless of prior dance experience or participation in the show. For those who are participating in the show, your choreographer will hold rehearsal once a week leading up to the show.

Our Shows

We have one show at the end of fall and spring semester. The fall show is a production of Tchaikovsky’s “The Nutcracker” and the spring show is a variety show that features ballet in the first act and any style of dance in the second act. The show dates are announced at the beginning of each semester during our kickoff meeting and will also be posted on our Facebook page.

How Do I Join?

You can contact the club to be placed on our email list to get the latest updates. At the beginning of the semester we ask you to fill out a piece placement form which tells us a little bit about your previous dance experience (if you have any), then we hold a placement audition in order to place everyone in the correct level dance for the show. If you miss the placement audition you can still come to our masterclass every week. Participation in the show is $25 per semester, but if you only want to come to the masterclass and not dance in the show you do not need to pay dues.

Who Can Choreograph?

Since the club is entirely student run the shows are also student choreographed. To choreograph you need to have participated in at least one previous Pitt Ballet Club show and then you can fill out the choreography form at the beginning of the semester along with your piece placement form.
